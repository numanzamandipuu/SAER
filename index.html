Society of Automotive Engineers RUET

SAER, initially established as the Society of Automotive Engineers RUET, is a non-political educational organization for engineering students in RUET. Principal emphasis is placed on transport industries. It is an affiliate society dedicated to the advancement of automotive knowledge and research in RUET as well as Bangladesh.
